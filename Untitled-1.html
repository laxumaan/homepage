Online Voting System

An online voting system is a digital platform that allows individuals to securely cast their votes remotely using the internet. It offers significant advantages, including increased accessibility for voters regardless of their location or physical abilities, improved efficiency and cost-effectiveness compared to traditional voting methods, and enhanced security measures to safeguard the integrity of the voting process.

It is a simple system created by undergraduate students of BCA at Tribhuvan University as their first project, under the guidance of their supervisor.
